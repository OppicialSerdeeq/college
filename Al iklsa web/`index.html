About Our College

Welcome to Al-Ikhlas College of Health Technology

Established with a vision to revolutionize healthcare education in Nigeria, Al-Ikhlas College of Health Technology, Tilden Fulani is dedicated to producing highly skilled professionals who can meet the evolving needs of the healthcare sector.

Our college combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, ensuring our graduates are job-ready and equipped to make meaningful contributions to healthcare delivery.

Accredited Programs

All our programs are fully accredited by relevant educational and professional bodies.

Experienced Faculty

Learn from industry professionals with years of practical healthcare experience.

Industry Partnerships

Strong ties with healthcare facilities for internships and job placements.

Our Programs

Health Information Management

Comprehensive training in managing patient health information, medical records, and healthcare data systems. Graduates are prepared for roles in hospitals, clinics, and health agencies.

Medical Laboratory Technology

Hands-on training in laboratory techniques, diagnostics, and medical testing procedures. Students learn to operate modern lab equipment and perform essential clinical tests.

Community Health Technology

Focused on public health, disease prevention, and community health education. Graduates work to improve health outcomes at the community level through education and intervention programs.
